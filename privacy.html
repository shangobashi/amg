Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-02

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact and data room request forms, including: company name, country, work email, phone number, title, mineral interest, intended engagement type, and any message content you provide. We do not collect payment information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through this website is used solely to route and respond to your inquiry, evaluate NDA and data room access requests, and communicate with qualified buyers about potential commercial engagement. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Inquiry and NDA request data is retained for up to 24 months from the date of submission, or until you request deletion, whichever is earlier. Upon request, we will delete all personal data associated with your inquiry within 30 days.

Cookies and Analytics

This website uses minimal cookies for functionality (form state) and basic analytics (page views, scroll depth, CTA interactions). We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Analytics data is aggregated and non-identifying by default.

Confidentiality of Commercial Information

All mineral assay data, geological information, financial projections, video evidence, and commercial terms shared through this website or the data room are treated as strictly confidential. Such information is disclosed only under executed non-disclosure agreements and solely to qualified counterparties.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or to request deletion of your data, please contact us through the contact form and include "Privacy Request" in your message.
