Responsible Sourcing

Transparency is a
prerequisite, not a differentiator.

We acknowledge the ESG challenges inherent in DRC artisanal mining. Our approach prioritises transparency, progressive improvement practices, and alignment with OECD Due Diligence Guidance. A documented due-diligence pack is available for qualified buyers.

Review Responsible Sourcing Policy
  • Mercury-aware operations with a documented phase-out commitment
  • Traceability from collection point to export port
  • OECD Due Diligence Guidance alignment (Annex I & II)
  • No child labour — adult artisanal teams, documented roster
  • Export documentation and chain-of-custody records available
