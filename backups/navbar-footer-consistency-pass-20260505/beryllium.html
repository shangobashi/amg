Market Opportunity

A thin market with high barriers to entry — and high margins for qualified sellers.

Beryllium is a specialised critical mineral used in aerospace, defence, telecommunications, and nuclear applications. The global market is thin and dominated by very few producers — primarily Materion Corporation (USA) and Chinese producers.

Premium-grade beryllium concentrate with documented chain of custody has potential for direct offtake arrangements with established processors. The key buyer concern is purity and provenance — the lab certificate from Alex Stewart directly addresses provenance.

End-Use Applications
  • Aerospace structural alloys and satellite components
  • Defence: missile guidance and optical systems
  • Telecom: 5G infrastructure and base station components
  • Nuclear: moderators and reflectors
  • Medical: X-ray windows, diagnostic equipment
