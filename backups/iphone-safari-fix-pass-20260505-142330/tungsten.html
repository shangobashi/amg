Market Context — Q2 2026

The most urgent opportunity in this data room.

Tungsten is classified as a critical mineral by the US, EU, Canada, Japan, and Australia. It is essential for hardmetal (carbide tooling), steel alloys, mining equipment, and emerging renewable energy applications.

The global tungsten market is facing acute supply pressure from Chinese export restrictions. China controls roughly 80% of global tungsten supply and has been tightening exports. WO₃ 67.04% is a high-grade concentrate by industry standards. Standard trading grade is 65% WO₃ minimum — this material is immediately bankable.

GTP / Trinity Metals (DRC) is the most immediate commercial target — they have established infrastructure on the Dar es Salaam corridor and are actively seeking non-Chinese tungsten supply to serve European and North American end-users.
