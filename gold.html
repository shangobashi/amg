Pathway to Activation

Three steps between this field reading and active gold marketing.

Step 1
Certified Fire Assay
Submit 500g sample to SGS, ALS, or Bureau Veritas in Kigali or Dar es Salaam. Fire assay with gravimetric finish. Cost: ~$80–150/sample. Turnaround: 5–7 days.
Step 2
Pre-finance Structure
Gold dust requires pre-finance to acquire from artisanal collectors. Trafigura, MKS, or a DRC-focused commodity trader can provide $500K–$2M pre-finance secured against documented gold.
Step 3
OECD Due Diligence
All DRC gold buyers require OECD Due Diligence Guidance compliance. Completed buyer due-diligence pack (traceability, chain of custody, ITSCI documentation) available in the data room.
  • Mercury-free processing (or documented phase-out plan) — mandatory for refinery acceptance
  • ITSCI membership or equivalent traceability scheme documentation
  • Gold weigh and purity certificate from first-sale transaction
